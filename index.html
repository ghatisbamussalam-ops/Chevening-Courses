Common Chevening Application Pitfalls

  • Course Duration: Courses must be 9-12 months long. Anything longer is ineligible.
  • Course Type: Only full-time, taught Master's degrees are eligible. MRes, PhDs, and part-time courses are not.
  • Location: The course must be based in the UK. Distance learning courses are not eligible, even if offered by a UK university.
  • London Branch Campuses: Be cautious with London branch campuses of universities based elsewhere. Ensure they are fully integrated and eligible.
  • Pre-sessional Courses: Costs for pre-sessional English courses are not covered.
  • Offer Deadline: You must have at least one unconditional offer uploaded to the Chevening portal by the official deadline.
